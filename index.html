Results

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}\) and \(k \in [0,2n]\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (k \not \in S+S)\}| = 2^{|n-k|} \cdot 3^{\lfloor ((n+1)-|n-k|)/2 \rfloor}\).   Nov 17 2021   Try it online!   Desmos

Other Results

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 1}\) and \(k \in [1,n]\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (k-1,k \not \in S+S)\}| = 2^{n-k} \cdot F_{k+2}\).   Oct 18 2021

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (|S|=n+1) \land (S+S = [0,2n])\}| = 1\).   Oct 28 2021

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 4}\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (|S|=n) \land (S+S = [0,2n])\}| = n-3\).   Oct 28 2021

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 6}\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (|S|=n-1) \land (S+S = [0,2n])\}| = \frac{(n-5)(n-2)}{2} - 1\).   Oct 28 2021

Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 9}\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (|S|=n-2) \land (S+S = [0,2n])\}| = \frac{(n-8)(n-3)(n-1)}{6} \).   Oct 28 2021

Notation

I use \([a,b]\) to denote the integer interval \(\{a,...,b\}\).
I use \(A+B\) to denote the sumset of \(A\) and \(B\), or \(\{a+b : (a \in A) \land (b \in B)\}\).
I use \(F_i\) to denote the \(i^{\text{th}}\) Fibonacci number, where \(F_0 = 0\), \(F_1 = 1\), and \(F_i = F_{i-1} + F_{i-2}\) for all \(i \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 2}\).
