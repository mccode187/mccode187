Given any \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}\) and \(k \in [0,2n]\), we have \(|\{S : (S \subseteq [0,n]) \land (k \in 2S)\}| = 2^{n+1} - 2^{|n-k|} \cdot 3^{\lfloor (n-|n-k|+1)/2 \rfloor}\).
